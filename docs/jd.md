What Nova actually is
Nova is GoComet’s platform transformation. Four pillars:
Workflow Orchestrator — Configurable, visual, multi-step business processes. Clients define their own approval chains, routing logic, and automation triggers through a drag-and-drop graph editor. YAML under the hood. React Flow on the screen. Zero hardcoded business logic — the engine is completely generic.
Agents Orchestrator — AI agents that handle analytics, document extraction, validation, monitoring, and recommendations. Built on LangGraph with a five-stage pipeline: scope resolution → context compilation → schema routing → plan + execute → evidence delivery. Every agent runs with governed business context, not ad-hoc prompting.
No-Code App Builder — Custom micro-apps attached to workflow steps. When a workflow node needs more than Approve/Reject — a rate comparison panel, a document review interface, an exception dashboard — it’s built here.
Data Layer — Unified ingestion from GoComet products, external sources, and client uploads. ClickHouse as the analytical store. Everything tagged, mapped, and queryable. Tenant-isolated by construction.

The hiring process
Round 1 — Take-home challenge (async): A real-world problem. Not a LeetCode puzzle. We’ll give you a messy logistics scenario and ask you to design a solution, build a working prototype, and write up your reasoning. Expect 3–4 hours.
Round 2 — Technical deep-dive (live, 60 min): Walk us through what you built. We’ll probe your design decisions, break your assumptions, and explore edge cases together. Then we’ll go into a live coding or system design extension.
Round 3 — Consulting simulation (live, 45 min): You’re the engineer. We’re the client. We’ll describe a messy approval process with contradictions and exceptions. Your job: ask the right questions, identify the core flow, and propose how to structure it.
Round 4 — Founder conversation (30 min): Culture, ambition, and fit. This is mutual — you’re evaluating us too.
